About us

A fun charity based in Leeds that aims to send fruit into schools

We are a fun charity based in Leeds, UK
Our aim is to educate young children in schools about nutrition and healthy eating

According to the World Health Organisation: In 2016, more than 1.9 billion adults, 18 years and older, were overweight. Of these over 650 million were obese. The key adipose, or fatty tissue, development period is between 3 and 5 years old. This can result in very young children becoming aware of their weight early on in life, which can hurt their self esteem.

We believe that it is very important to make children aware of the risks of not eating a healthy, nutritious diet early on so that they can become empowered to make good decisions about their own health and feel happy about themselves.

We hope that with a fun and friendly attitude we will be able to get kids more interested in healthy eating at a younger age, before weight problems can develop in adulthood!

Our Vision

Here at Fruit Salad we are a group of university students dedicated to giving school children the best start at life.
We believe the best way to achieve this aim is to fill their brains with nutrients, so our food science department put together
a list of the most nutitious fruits for younger students
and the rest of us head into to schools and tell the children the merits of eating fruit.

We would like to invite you to join in our mission and give the greatest helping hand to those who need it
